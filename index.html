MBTI Profile

INFP

My MBTI is INFP. INFPs are deeply creative and idealistic, driven by their values and a desire to make a meaningful impact. They possess a strong sense of empathy, a deep understanding of others, and a constant drive for personal growth, which helps them connect with people and ideas in a unique and thoughtful way.


I love exploring new ideas and finding creative ways to bring them to life. Whether it's through data, design, or solving problems, I enjoy working on things that feel meaningful. I like taking my time to understand things deeply and always look for ways to improve.


I can be quite independent, but I also enjoy bouncing ideas off others and learning from different perspectives. I believe that even small details can make a big difference, and I try to create work that feels thoughtful and purposeful.
