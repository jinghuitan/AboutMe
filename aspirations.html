Short-term Goals

In the short term, I plan to reflect on my career journey and evaluate the next steps as I approach graduation. I aspire to take on more leadership roles, managing teams and projects that use data to solve complex problems.

Ultimately, I hope to integrate my technical career with my personal interests, like climbing and crocheting, creating a fulfilling balance between my work and passions that allows me to pursue a well-rounded and meaningful life.
